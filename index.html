What does the company do?

DV8 is a South African Information and communication technology headquatered company which is fully black-owned by South Afracan enterise. The company operates at a scale that allow them to offer cost-effective cutting edge solutions, and their products and services are superior in quality.

The successful of the company is clearly seen by the fact that it was incorporated in 2006 now they are already making the changes in South Africa, Southern African Development Community and Africa at large while managing world-class, profitable and sustainable operation.

DV8 focuses in Advanced Infrastracture, Software Services, Cyber Security, Managed Services, Business Consulting and Information Technology Products/Solutions. They are most reliable in delivering excellence, efficiency, creativity, integrity, and transparency of their services.

The company really cares about the team they have and appreciate their skills and experience they bring to the company which can make anyone love to work for them.
